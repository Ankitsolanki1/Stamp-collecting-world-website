    • The purpose of this STAMP COLLECTING website is to provide

    general information for visitors that may be interested in collecting stamps.

    To provide the specialized information needs of more advanced stamp collectors and philatelists and to create this website.

    Postage stamps not only represent the development of letter mail systems over last two centuries

    also reflect full range of history from beginnings of global civilize

    It is my love of history, as well as my love of philately, that drove me to collect stamps (about 60 years ago)

    and to create this website (almost 10 years ago).





    The focus of most of the collecting categories in this website is classical (1840-1900) and early modern (1901 to 1935) philately of Europe North America.

    In the beginning, I had envisioned a worldwide philatelic site, but it became evident that maintaining a site of scope, for all the countries existed since 1840.

    After almost 10 years of development and about 965 content pages, website Collecting Categories section includes about every stamp-issuing country in Europe and America.

    In the very near future, I will be able to move into the improvement and maintenance phase. That is, being able to begin revising and improving the text content

    Some of older categories and being able to start adding new Information and Collecting page content to existing website categories, something that I've wanted to do for long time.
